About Us

BetterWorld was created in 2003 by Jane Doe to support and raise awareness towards people with schizophrenia. Our mission is broad and varies from information on the underlying conditions developed by schizophrenic persons, its effects on the behavior and its consequences in everyday life to risk prevention on psychotic episodes.Our goal is to bring together the community of schizophrenic individuals and their families to create a free space for sharing, communicating and exchanging around schizophrenia. If about 200 members in total, we would love to welcome you in the family. We offer a subscription to our newsletter emailed monthly. If you are interested please complete the form below and do not forget to press submit. Last but not least, check out our events in your city and do not forget to register as the spots are limited.

What is schizophrenia ?

It is serious mental condition of a type involving a breakdown in the relation between thought, emotion, and behaviour, leading to faulty perception, inappropriate actions and feelings, withdrawal from reality and personal relationships into fantasy and delusion, and a sense of mental fragmentation.
